AXIOS

Constantinople, 4th Century AD

You are a catechumen in the age of Saint John Chrysostom. Explore the Queen of Cities, serve its people, encounter saints, and prepare for baptism in a historical adventure shaped by Holy Orthodoxy.

Lived Theology

Learn through action, relationships, and moral struggle — not exposition dumps.

Historical Immersion

Constantinople rendered with the texture of late antique urban life: churches, markets, harbors, feast days.

Human-Scale Drama

Stakes that feel personal, moral, and communal. Souls before spectacle.
